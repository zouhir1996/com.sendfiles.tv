Before you write

Including these details helps us assist you faster:

  • Phone model and iOS or Android version
  • TV or device type (and browser name, if you know it)
  • What you tried and what happened (screenshots welcome)

Quick tips

  • Same Wi‑Fi — Your phone and TV (or other device) should be on the same home network.
  • Use a browser on the TV — Open the link or scan the QR code with a web browser that can load pages on your local network.
  • VPNs and guest networks — VPNs or “guest” Wi‑Fi can block local connections. Try turning the VPN off or using the main network.
  • App in background — Sharing may stop when you leave the app; open the app again to start a new session if needed.
