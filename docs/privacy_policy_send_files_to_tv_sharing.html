Privacy Policy

Send Files to TV Sharing
Last updated: February 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the developer of Send Files to TV Sharing (“we”, “us”) handles information when you use the app. If you have questions, contact us at moufarijamix50@gmail.com.

1. Summary

We respect your privacy. The app is built so you can share files from your phone to other devices on your Wi‑Fi (for example, using a browser on your TV). The sharing feature is intended to work locally on your network, not as a cloud upload to our servers. We use advertising through Google, which may process certain data as described below.

2. Information the app processes

2.1 Files you choose
When you select files (including photos or videos you pick through the system picker), the app reads them on your device to offer them over a temporary local connection. We do not intend for those files to be uploaded to our servers for that feature.

2.2 Network information (sharing)
To help you share on your home network, the app may use local network or device network details (for example, to show an address or QR code). This supports the sharing feature on your Wi‑Fi.

2.3 App settings
We may store preferences on your device (for example, using on-device storage) so the app can remember choices such as onboarding or settings.

2.4 Advertising (Google AdMob)
We use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to show ads. Google and its partners may collect and use identifiers and related data to deliver and measure ads, consistent with your device settings and Google’s policies. You can learn more in Google’s privacy documentation and your device’s privacy and advertising controls.

2.5 Ad configuration
The app may download a small configuration file over HTTPS (for example, ad placement configuration). That is a standard web request and is not used to upload your selected files.

3. How we use information

  • To provide file selection and local Wi‑Fi sharing.
  • To keep in-app settings on your device.
  • To show and measure ads through Google AdMob and related services.

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. Google (AdMob) and advertising partners may process data under their own policies when ads are shown. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and integrity.

5. Data retention

On-device preferences remain until you delete the app or clear app data, depending on your device. Advertising partners retain data according to their policies.

6. Security

Sharing is designed to occur on networks you control, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Use trusted networks and only share content you are comfortable sending to nearby devices.

7. Your choices

Depending on your region, you may have privacy rights regarding personal data. You may also control personalized advertising through your device settings and Google’s ad settings. To contact us about this policy or privacy requests, email moufarijamix50@gmail.com.

9. International users
